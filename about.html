About Me

My Story

My journey began back in 2015, where the first language we were taught in CS 101 was Pascal. For as archaic as that language is, I found great interest in the possibilities of programming and decided to pursue being a developer. By 2018, I had completed all the CS classes offered at my high school and decided to commute to a nearby college so that I could keep improving my skillsets before setting off for college.

To continue my studies, I attended Marquette University for their accelerated degree program with a plan to get my B.S in Computer Science and M.S in Data Science within five years. This part of my journey took me in the most unexpected directions. From working in ASM and C to create a functioning operating system, all the way to implementing AI techniques to develop a Connect 4 agent, the skillsets I've grown have allowed me to be even more passionate about what I do now and eager to see what I'll do next.

Alongside the skills I've gained through my courses, I have been fortunate enough to have had some great internships. The mentors I've had and projects I've worked on have given me guidance for where I should aim my career as well as an emphasis on how to use data to deliver value to a business.

More information about my experience, internships, and projects can be found below or on my resume.
